About Me

As I transition from a career in healthcare to the world of web design, I’m driven by a passion for creating 'something from nothing' and the opportunity to craft engaging user experiences. Web design offers a unique blend of creativity and problem-solving that resonates with my desire for a career that balances work and life while allowing me to innovate and express myself.

I’m drawn to the autonomy that comes with front-end design—having the freedom to bring my ideas to life while collaborating with others under some guidance. The values of the web design community, such as open-source collaboration and the communal support for solving coding challenges, are aspects I deeply admire. I thrive in environments where creativity is celebrated and where there’s a shared commitment to helping each other succeed.

I’m excited about the possibilities this new path holds and eager to contribute to a team where my skills and enthusiasm for design can make a meaningful impact.
